Milo

A malty Aussie Kiwi classic

Enjoy with a bikkie

Ingredients

  • 3 tsp Milo
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Milk
  • Hot Water

Method

  1. Boil water.
  2. Put Milo and sugar in a mug and add enough milk to cover the dry ingredients.
  3. Add hot water to the desired level and stir.
  4. Enjoy your hot milo.
